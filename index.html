Post Malone's Mother

Feburary 26th

Raising an Ancient Alien

I often wonder what our lord's mother felt like when she gave birth to the king. I wondered what feelings she felt as she realized she was raising a savior.

I didn't have to wonder for long. When I gave birth to little Post Malone, I simply knew he was from somewhere else. He had a look in his eyes of deep, deep desire to commit suicide. My beautiful, brand-new, infant son longing to end his suffering once again in the simulation. It was a lot for a mother to take on.
